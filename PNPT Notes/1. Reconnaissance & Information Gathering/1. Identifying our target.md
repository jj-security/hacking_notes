## Identifying our target

Go to [bugcrowd](https://bugcrowd.com) or other bug bounty sites and pick a client.

Make sure to stay within the confines of the scope. Some scopes are scanning and enumeration based (can't fuzz for subdomains/VHOST lookups) and some are IP/DNS based (Can't go to admin.x.com, but can go to test.x.com)
